This page is where you will find a large selection of Flash game files, spread out between 3 different archives.

For the Flash Game Emulator to work you need files that look like this "yourgame.swf"

Hover over "Flash Games v" and click on "Flash Emulator" to access the emulator. Press load file and select your .swf file. Some files have fullscreen ability, some don't. It's a case by case basis.

Look through all of the archives before you make a game request. Some of the archives may have what you're looking for. To get the files, click on any of the "Archive #" links. It will take you to a GitHub page. Click the green "< > Code" button. In the menu that appears, click "Download ZIP". Once it downloads to your device, go to the "Flash Emulator" page and follow the instructions above.
